Introduction

Game System is developed and operated by Billionz Gaming. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Game System application and related services.

By using Game System, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

02

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may collect information provided by Google, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile information

This information is used to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Identify you across Billionz Gaming services
  • Provide access to account features
  • Provide VIP benefits and personalized services

User Information

We may store information related to your account, including:

  • Account identifiers
  • Username or profile information
  • VIP status
  • Access permissions
  • Service-related data
  • Purchase or entitlement information

This information allows us to provide and manage Game System features.

03

Device Information

Game System may collect limited device information, including:

  • Device model
  • Device manufacturer
  • Android version
  • Device identifiers where required
  • Installed Billionz Gaming applications

Installed application information is used only to:

  • Detect supported Billionz Gaming products
  • Improve compatibility
  • Provide access to related services

We do not use installed application information to monitor personal activity.

04

Firebase and Google Services

Game System uses Google services, including Firebase and Google Sign-In. These services may process information to provide:

  • Account authentication
  • Secure database storage
  • Cloud communication
  • Application security features

Information handled by these services is processed according to Google's privacy practices.

05

Advertising

If advertising services are added to Game System, third-party advertising providers may collect information such as:

  • Advertising identifiers
  • Device information
  • Usage information

This information may be used for:

  • Displaying advertisements
  • Measuring advertisement performance
  • Providing rewarded advertisements
06

Payments and VIP Services

Game System may provide paid services, including VIP features. Payment information may be processed to:

  • Verify purchases
  • Grant purchased benefits
  • Maintain access to paid features

We do not store payment card information directly unless required for providing a service.

07

Permissions We Use

Internet Permission

Used for communication between the application and Game System servers.

Network Access Permission

Used to check network availability and connection status.

Storage Permission

Used for downloading and saving user-requested files.

Notification Permission

Used for important updates, download progress, and service notifications.

Permissions are only requested when required for application functionality.

08

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Your information may only be shared with trusted services required to operate Game System, including:

  • Google Firebase
  • Google Sign-In
  • Advertising providers when enabled
  • Payment providers when required
09

Data Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. Communication between Game System and our servers uses secure connections where applicable.

However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

10

User Rights

Users may request:

Requests can be submitted through our support email.

11

Game System is designed for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

12

We keep information only for as long as necessary to provide services, maintain accounts, prevent abuse, and meet legal requirements.
